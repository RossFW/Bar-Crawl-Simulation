Model Assumptions
  1. Groups always travel bar 1 → 7; no backtracking.
  2. Late arrivers placed at whichever bar they'd have reached walking from bar 1.
  3. Dwell time ~ Normal(mean, σ=8 min), min 5 min.
  4. At each departure, ~skip% chance to jump an extra bar (max 2).
  5. Info shared only when two+ groups are dwelling at the same bar simultaneously.
  6. Once tipped off, a group walks directly to host bar (skips everything in between).
  7. Host is stationary at one bar for the full simulation.
  8. Groups never split or merge.
  9. Simulation ends at 180 min or when all groups are found.
  10. Walking times between bars are fixed estimates from Google Maps.
