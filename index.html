Model Assumptions
  1. Groups start at a random bar and prefer visiting adjacent uncleared bars.
  2. 15% chance per departure to skip adjacent and pick any uncleared bar.
  3. Dwell time ~ Normal(mean, σ=8 min), clamped to [15, 60] min.
  4. Groups share which bars they've cleared when they recognize each other.
  5. If a group clears 6 of 7 bars, they deduce the host's location.
  6. Once tipped/deduced, a group finishes their drink then beelines to host.
  7. Host is stationary at one bar for the full simulation.
  8. Groups never split or merge.
  9. Simulation ends at 300 min or when all groups are found.
  10. Walking times between bars are fixed estimates from Google Maps.
